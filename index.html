Why use a password generator?

Weak or reused passwords are one of the most common causes of account breaches. A randomly generated password with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols is significantly harder to crack than a human-chosen word or phrase.

  • Use a unique password for every account
  • Aim for at least 16 characters
  • Include symbols and numbers
  • Use a password manager to store them safely
  • Enable two-factor authentication where available

Where to use strong passwords

  • Web hosting control panels (DirectAdmin, cPanel, Plesk)
  • WordPress admin accounts
  • SSH and server logins
  • Database accounts (MySQL, MariaDB)
  • Business email accounts
  • Domain registrar accounts
  • VPS server root passwords
  • FTP and SFTP accounts
